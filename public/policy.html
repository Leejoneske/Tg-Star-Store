4.2 The 21-Day Holding Period Explained

The mandatory 21-day holding period is a critical security measure designed to protect users, StarStore, and the integrity of our marketplace. This policy aligns with Telegram's official policies and industry best practices.

Security Rationale

  1. Fraud Prevention: Allows sufficient time to identify and investigate potentially fraudulent transactions, stolen accounts, or unauthorized Star transfers
  2. Telegram Policy Compliance: Mirrors Telegram's own 21-day holding requirement for Stars transactions to ensure platform-wide consistency
  3. Chargeback Protection: Provides adequate time for payment reversals or disputes to surface before funds are released
  4. Account Verification: Enables thorough verification of seller account legitimacy and transaction history
  5. Regulatory Compliance: Satisfies financial regulatory requirements for transaction monitoring and suspicious activity detection

4.3 Selling Process

  1. Initiate Sell Order: Contact @TgStarStore_bot and select the "Sell Stars" option
  2. Specify Amount: Enter the quantity of Stars you wish to sell (minimum 50 Stars)
  3. Rate Confirmation: Review and confirm the current exchange rate of $0.009 per Star and estimated USDT payout
  4. Wallet Address Provision: Provide your USDT wallet address (TON or TRON network)
  5. Wallet Verification: Confirm wallet address accuracy (transactions cannot be reversed)
  6. Star Transfer: Send Stars via Telegram payment to our designated account
  7. Order Tracking: Receive unique order ID and track status throughout the 21-day period
  8. Payment Processing: After 21 days, USDT is sent to your wallet within 24 hours

  • Network Compatibility: Ensure your wallet supports the selected network (TON or TRON TRC-20)
  • Address Format: Provide complete, unmodified wallet addresses without additional characters or spaces
