Selling Telegram Stars

Convert your Telegram Stars to USDT with StarStore's secure selling platform.

Selling Process

  1. 1. Navigate to the Sell page in StarStore
  2. 2. Enter amount (50-80,000 Stars per transaction)
  3. 3. Manually enter your USDT (TON Network) wallet address
  4. 4. Confirm the transaction
  5. 5. Stars held for 21 days for security verification
  6. 6. USDT payment sent after holding period

Important Notes

  • • Minimum sell amount: 50 Stars
  • • Maximum per transaction: 80,000 Stars
  • • Wallet must be on TON network with no memo/tag
  • • Transaction history available in History page

Why There's a 21-Day Wait After Selling Stars

When you sell Telegram Stars, your stars are held for 21 days before payment is made. Here's why:

  • • Protecting the Stars Ecosystem: Keeps the system stable and fair for everyone
  • • Aligned with Telegram's Policy: Follows similar standards to remain compliant
  • • Preventing Fraud: Allows us to confirm transactions were valid and protect users

This policy ensures a trusted platform that rewards real users fairly.

Referral Program

How Referrals Work

A referred user becomes active when they:
