Selling Telegram Stars

Convert your Telegram Stars to USDT with StarStore's secure selling platform.

Selling Process

  1. 1. Navigate to the Sell page in StarStore
  2. 2. Enter the amount of Stars to sell (50-80,000 per transaction)
  3. 3. Provide your USDT (TON Network) wallet address
  4. 4. Confirm the transaction
  5. 5. Stars will be held for 21 days for security verification
  6. 6. USDT payment sent after holding period

Important Notes

  • • Minimum sell amount: 50 Stars
  • • Maximum per transaction: 80,000 Stars
  • • 21-day holding period for fraud prevention
  • • Payments made in USDT (TON Network or TRON20)
  • • Transaction history available in your History page

Security Measures

The 21-day holding period is mandatory to prevent refund fraud and protect both buyers and sellers. This period allows us to verify transactions and prevent losses from chargebacks.
