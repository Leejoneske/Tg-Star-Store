# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for all contributors, users, and community members, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

All participants in the StarStore community are expected to:

- **Be Respectful**: Treat all individuals with kindness and respect in all interactions
- **Be Constructive**: Provide helpful feedback and engage in constructive discussions
- **Be Inclusive**: Welcome and support people of all backgrounds and experiences
- **Be Professional**: Maintain professionalism in all communications
- **Listen Actively**: Give your full attention to others' perspectives
- **Take Responsibility**: Own your mistakes and learn from feedback
- **Report Issues**: Bring concerns to appropriate channels rather than public blame

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable and will not be tolerated:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Offensive comments related to personal characteristics or beliefs
- Deliberate intimidation or threats
- Unwanted sexual attention or advances
- Publishing private information without consent
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Violence, threats of violence, or violent language
- Spam or commercial promotion without permission
- Repeated violations despite warnings

## Consequences

Unacceptable behavior will result in:

1. **Private Warning**: First offense receives a private, written warning
2. **Public Warning**: Repeated violations result in a public warning
3. **Temporary Ban**: Temporary ban from community (repositories, discussions, etc.)
4. **Permanent Ban**: Serious or repeated violations result in permanent ban

The severity of consequences depends on the nature and context of the violation.

## Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:

1. **Do not engage** directly with the person engaging in the behavior
2. **Report immediately** to the project maintainers
3. **Provide details** including:
   - Description of the incident
   - Time and location (URLs for online incidents)
   - Individuals involved
   - Witnesses (if any)
   - Context of the incident

Email reports to project maintainers with subject: "Code of Conduct Violation Report"

## Confidentiality

All reports will be:
- Treated with confidentiality
- Investigated thoroughly
- Handled fairly and impartially
- Kept private (unless the reporter agrees otherwise)

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project spaces (repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Community events (online meetings, conferences)
- Official social media accounts
- Events organized under the StarStore name
- Official project communications

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for:
- Clarifying expectations for acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate corrective action
- Removing comments, commits, code, etc. that violate this Code
- Banning contributors who fail to follow this Code
